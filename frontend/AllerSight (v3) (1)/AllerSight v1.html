02  · For kitchens The prep line, in sight

What a peanut touches,
AllerSight remembers.

Line cooks wear lightweight glasses. A ceiling camera covers the whole pass. Together they identify every visible ingredient in real time, and — crucially — maintain a live contamination memory across hands, knives, pans, tongs and boards.

Wash a knife, the chain breaks. Rinse your hands, the chain breaks. Until then, the knife is still "peanut." The glove is still "shellfish." Every plate that touches it inherits the risk.

Contamination memory
A graph of every surface and tool, with its current allergen state. Persistent across service.
Wash-to-clear
Hand-washing and tool-rinsing are detected visually — and reset the object's state.
Glasses + ceiling cam
Works with Meta-style wearables or a fixed fisheye over the prep line. One model, two eyes.
Proof on tap
Every action is signed and archived — ready for insurers, inspectors, or a lawsuit you'd rather avoid.
