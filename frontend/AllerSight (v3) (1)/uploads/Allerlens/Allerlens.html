More than allergens

Also a quiet coach for your diet goals.

Every scan doubles as a food log. AllerVision estimates calories and macros from the same photo it reads your allergens from — so hitting a goal doesn't mean a second app, a second tap, or a second thought.

  • Calories Portion-aware estimates for restaurant plates, packaged foods, and home cooking. Within ±8% of a dietitian's weigh-in, on average.
  • Macros Protein, carbs and fat tallied into your daily ring. Set a target — cut, maintain, build — and watch the gap close across the day.
  • Goals Pick a plan or build your own: Mediterranean, high-protein, low-FODMAP, 16:8, or custom ratios. The app coaches toward it without nagging.
  • Micros Flag low iron, vitamin D, B12 and fiber weeks before your body does — and suggest allergen-safe foods to patch the gaps.

The lens, always on

It runs in the background.
And lives in your glasses.

Open AllerVision once. From then on, it's a small green dot in your status bar — or a soft amber glow in your HUD. Through your phone camera or your Meta-style smart glasses, every dish that enters view is silently identified, matched to your profile, and announced with a single notification telling you plainly: eat, or don't.
